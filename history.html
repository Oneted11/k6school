Mission Statement

The mission of K6 High SChool is to seek exceptionally promising students
of all backgrounds from across the nation and around the world and to educate them, through
mental discipline and social experience,
to develop their intellectual ,moral ,civic,and creative capacities to the fullest.

The aim of this education is the cultivation of citizens with a rich awareness of our heritage
to lead and serve in every sphere of human activity.
